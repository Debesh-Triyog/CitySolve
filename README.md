# 🌆 CitySolve Nepal  
### Community Innovation Hub for Real Urban Change

https://github.com/Debesh-Triyog/CitySolve.git
Welcome to **CitySolve Nepal** — a space where ideas turn into action, and communities come together to solve real problems in our cities 🇳🇵

---

## 💛 Our Mission

We believe that **everyone has the power to create change**.

CitySolve Nepal is built to:
- 🌱 Empower young innovators and local communities  
- 🤝 Encourage collaboration across cities  
- 💡 Turn everyday problems into impactful solutions  

From air pollution in Kathmandu to water scarcity in Lalitpur — **your ideas matter**.

---

## 🚀 What This Platform Does

CitySolve Nepal allows users to:

### 🔍 Explore Problems
Discover real urban challenges across different cities in Nepal.

### 🛠️ Share Solutions
Submit your own ideas to tackle issues like:
- Environment 🌿  
- Waste ♻️  
- Transport 🚗  
- Water 💧  
- Infrastructure 🏗️  

### 🗳️ Support Ideas
Upvote and explore community-driven solutions.

### 🌍 Build Together
Connect with students, changemakers, NGOs, and thinkers.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. **Identify a Problem**  
   Browse challenges in your city  

2. **Collaborate & Innovate**  
   Think, design, and refine your idea  

3. **Submit & Grow**  
   Share your solution and gain support
